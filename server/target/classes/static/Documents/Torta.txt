Torta is a culinary term that can, depending on the cuisine, refer to cakes, pies, flatbreads, sandwiches, or omelettes.
Usually, it refers to:

cake or pie in South America, much of Europe, and southern Philippines
flatbread in Spain
a type of sandwich in Mexico
a type of omelette in northern Tagalog-speaking areas of the Philippines.’Torth’- Welsh for ‘loaf’ is of the same derivation (Latin: torta)

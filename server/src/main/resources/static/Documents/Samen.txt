Samen (Persian: سامن, also Romanized as Sāmen and Sāman) is a city in, and the capital of, Samen District of Malayer County, Hamadan province, Iran. The local language in Samen is Persian.At the 2006 census, its population was 4,025 in 1,207 households. The following census in 2011 counted 4,426 people in 1,269 households. The latest census in 2016 showed a population of 3,873 people in 1,268 households.

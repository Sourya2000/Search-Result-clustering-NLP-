Wesley Tyler Glass (born October 26, 1992), known professionally as Wheezy, is an American record producer and songwriter. He has worked with artists such as Lil Uzi Vert, Young Thug, Lil Baby, Future, King Von, 21 Savage, Gunna, Nav, Travis Scott, Drake, Bankroll Mafia, Trippie Redd, Juice Wrld, Rich the Kid, Meek Mill, Lil Durk, among others.
Wheezy has produced many notable songs throughout his career, such as the 2018 songs "Yes Indeed" by Lil Baby and Drake, and "Going Bad" by Meek Mill featuring Drake.
Wheezy solely produced all songs from American rapper Gunna's debut extended play, Drip or Drown (2017). He later served as executive producer alongside American record producer Turbo and American rapper Young Thug for Gunna's debut studio album, Drip or Drown 2 (2019) and his second studio album, Wunna (2020), also producing the majority of the songs from both albums.As a songwriter he worked on the songs "iMi" and "We" by Bon Iver from their fourth studio album i, i (2019). Wheezy primarily produced Canadian rapper Nav's commercial mixtape, Emergency Tsunami (2020).
